Another one of Miyazaki's masterpieces is Dark Souls 3. Dark souls 3 is the ultimate culmination of the first 2 and is widely considered the best of the 3. The game has a nice finality to it as it probably will the the final dark souls game, and the game makes you feel like the journey is coming to an end. To add onto that, dark souls 3 has some of the best bosses in the series like "slave knight gael" and "sister friede" while the worst bosses in the game are still enjoyable even if they are easy.
